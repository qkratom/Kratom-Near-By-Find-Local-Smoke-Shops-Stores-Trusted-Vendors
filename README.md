## Understanding Kratom and Its Growing Popularity

**Kratom** (*Mitragyna speciosa*) is a tropical evergreen tree native to Southeast Asia, particularly Thailand, Malaysia, and Indonesia. The leaves contain naturally occurring alkaloids, primarily **mitragynine** and **7-hydroxymitragynine**, which interact with the body's opioid receptors. Traditional users in Southeast Asia have consumed kratom leaves for centuries, typically by chewing fresh leaves or brewing them into tea.

The herb has gained significant traction in Western countries, particularly in the United States, where millions of Americans now use kratom for various purposes. According to industry estimates, the kratom market has experienced substantial growth, with users seeking alternatives for pain management, energy enhancement, and mood support.
